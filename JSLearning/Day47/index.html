Goku Super Saiyan

Power Level: 9000

Goku is a Saiyan originally sent to Earth as an infant with the mission to destroy it. However, after suffering a head injury, he loses his destructive nature and becomes one of Earth's greatest defenders. Known for his cheerful personality and insatiable appetite, Goku is also renowned for his incredible fighting skills and ability to achieve various powerful transformations, including the iconic Super Saiyan form.
